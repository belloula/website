About Our Company

Health Zone is a website about Health institutions in the batna territory.

Our focus is on health promotion as well as returning care to a full wellness framework. We are driven by our primary goal to play an active and supportive role in every person's life. As a result, we present our website. To help our customers live a better life..

in health zone the user finds information about various services, such as providing places of hospitals, pharmacies, and other medical facilities through the maps page, in addition we are placing the basic emergency numbers to help you at your time of need. . .

Our History

Health Zone was created by a group of undergraduate students, an end-of-study project in the major, Information Systems and Software Engineering, in their graduation year 2021, The vision of making the brand name synonymous with accessible healthcare birthed the existence of Health Zone.

H Zone An eaisier way to your health

With our strategic plan, in a short span of time, Health Zone will quickly positioned itself as a well-known name in the Health care Industry within the DZ.
